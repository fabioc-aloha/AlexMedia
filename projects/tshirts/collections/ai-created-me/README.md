# AI-Generated Meta Collection

## Concept

Self-referential, meta humor — merch that openly admits it was designed by AI. Appeals to tech-savvy audiences, AI enthusiasts, developers, and anyone who appreciates the irony of wearing AI-generated art. The text on each piece varies — that's the point. Every shirt is a different riff on the same joke.

## Tagline

> "Honestly, I didn't even pick the font."
