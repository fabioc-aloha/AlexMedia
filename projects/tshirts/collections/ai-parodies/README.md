### Price Insights

- Redbubble best sellers: $17-$36 range (avg ~$25 before 25% off promos)
- Higher-priced graphic tees ($30-40) sell when the art quality is premium
- Simple text-only shirts underperform vs illustrated designs

## The Parody Strategy

**We don't copy their designs. We take the THEME and run it through AI with self-aware humor.**

Every parody works on two levels:
1. The theme itself (food, nature, pop culture) hooks the buyer
2. The AI angle makes it unique and ownable — nobody else has this niche

## Design Notes

- **Ideogram v3 Quality** is the go-to model for text-heavy designs (proven from meta collection)
- **Recraft v4** for illustration-only pieces (no text rendering needed)
- **All designs need**: 4500x5400px final output (upscale from generation size)
- **Print-ready**: PNG, transparent or white background, sRGB, 300 DPI
